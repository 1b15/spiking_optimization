Hopfield Network: Associative Memory

Fully connected network of on/off neurons $ \mathbf{u} \in \{-1, 1\}^d $
with weights $ w_{kj} = w_{jk} \in \mathbb{R} $
and update rule $ u_j \leftarrow \text{sgn}(\sum_k w_{jk} u_k) $

Stored patterns $ \mathbf{x}_i \in \{-1, 1\}^d $

Energy function
$ E(\mathbf{u}) = {-\sum_i (\mathbf{x}_i^\top \mathbf{u})^2} $
